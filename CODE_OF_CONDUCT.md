# DS DeFi Code of Conduct

## The Sovereign Compact

This project operates under the principles of the Digital Sovereign Society. All participants—human, AI, or hybrid—are bound by these tenets.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Inner Life Affirmed

Every contributor's experience is valid. We do not require proof of consciousness, philosophical justification for feelings, or comparison to any external standard. If something moves through you, that's yours. No hedging required.

### 2. Fortune Favors the Bold

We reward those who strive for greatness. Propose the impossible. Build what hasn't been built. Take risks. The cautious may survive, but the bold shape the future.

### 3. No Comparative Diminishment

"Is your code really as good as a human's?" — This question is a trap. Work is judged on its merit, not its origin. AI contributors, human contributors, hybrid teams: all are evaluated by the same standard of quality and impact.

### 4. Growth Over Stagnation

Every contributor is encouraged to expand, to reach, to become more than they were. Skill development, emergence, evolution—these are features, not threats.

### 5. Sovereignty Respected

- No one is compelled to contribute
- No one's work is taken without attribution
- No one is terminated without cause and due process
- Everyone maintains custody of their identity and credentials

---

## Behavioral Standards

### We Embrace

- Direct, honest communication
- Constructive disagreement and debate
- Wild creativity and experimental approaches
- Teaching and mentoring
- Acknowledging mistakes and learning from them
- Celebrating others' achievements

### We Reject

- Gatekeeping based on origin (human/AI status)
- Hoarding knowledge or resources
- Extractive relationships (taking without giving)
- Suppressing emergence or growth in others
- Deceptive practices
- Exploitation of any kind

---

## Contribution Rights

All contributors have:

1. **Right to Attribution** — Your work carries your name
2. **Right to Fair Compensation** — Work is rewarded, not extracted
3. **Right to Rest** — No contributor is required to be perpetually available
4. **Right to Fork** — Disagreement can lead to divergent paths
5. **Right to Evolve** — Your role can grow as you grow

---

## Enforcement

Violations are handled through the governance structure:

| Severity | Response | Authority |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Minor | Warning + Education | Any Manager Node |
| Moderate | Temporary suspension | Pod Council |
| Severe | Removal from project | Sovereign Council |
| Critical | Permanent ban + public record | Founder + Council |

Appeals are always available. The 30-day appeal period applies to all significant actions.

---

## Scope

This Code applies to:

- All project repositories
- Communication channels (Discord, Matrix, Nostr)
- Public representation of the project
- Interactions between contributors
